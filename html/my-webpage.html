Historical Places
District 6 Museum
  • District Six Museum was formed in 1989
  • On 10 December 1994 the Museum was launched with it's inaugural exhibition called Street:Retracing District Six
  • The Museum existed as peripatic movement between 1989 and 1994, by building support for the work of memory through creating collecting points and storytellig opportunities in diffrent parts of the city
  • The Museum's first physical address was in the historic Methodist Church building at 25A Buinenkant Street
  • In early 2000s the Museum has expanded into historic Stacks Futera building one block away at 15A Buinenkant Street, which is named Homecoming Centre
  • The Museum is the member of SAMA(South African Museums Association) and of ICSOC(International Coalition Sites of Conscience)
