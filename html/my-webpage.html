District Six Museum
  • District Six Museum was formed in 1989
  • On 10 December 1994 the Museum exhibition called Street:Retracing District Six
  • The Museum existed as a peripatic movement between 1989 and 1994, by building support for the work of memory through creating collecting points and storytelling opportunities in different parts of the city
  • The Museum's first physical Methodist Church building at 25A Buitnenkat Street
  • In early 2000s the Museum was expanded into historic Sacks Futera building one block away at 15A Buitnenkat Street, which was named Homecoming Center
  • The Museum ia member of SAMA(South African Museum Association) and of ICSOC(International Coalition of Sites of Conscience)
Iziko Museum
  • The Iziko South African Museum is a South African national museum located in Cape Town
  • Iziko is an IsiXhosa word meaning 'hearth'
  • Iziko Museum operates 11 national museum in Cape Town
  • The museum was founded in 1825, the first in the country
  • It is been in the present site in the company's garden since 1897
  • The museum houses importnat African zoology, paleontology and archaelogy collection
Iziko Slave Lodge
  • The Slave Lodge was built in 1679
  • The slave lodge was the largest slave holding at the Cape until 1806
  • It housed an average of 476 inmates at a time consisting slaves, convicts and mentally ill
  • Today the Slave Lodge is a museum, managed by Iziko Museum of South Africa
  • The building was renamed the slave lodge on Heritage Day, 1998git
